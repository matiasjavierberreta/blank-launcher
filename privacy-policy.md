# Privacy Policy — Blank Launcher

*Last updated: March 2026*

Blank Launcher is a minimal Android launcher developed by Blank Apps.

## Data Collection

Blank Launcher does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The app does not require an internet connection to function.

## Permissions

Blank Launcher requests the following permissions:
- **Query all packages**: Required to display your installed apps list.
- **Package usage stats**: Required for the distraction app blocking feature.

## In-App Purchases

Blank Launcher offers a one-time purchase (Blank Pro) processed entirely through Google Play. We do not store or process any payment information.
